Multiple times people told me I should have a personal website... and I kinda wanted it... so after years I did it.

In life, I build software for hardware.

Ever since I was a child, I have been building 3D printers, robotic arms, CNC machines, and various hardware pieces out of passion. I always struggled to prepare my designs for assembly and production. I would typically end up wasting 4kg of PLA on a couple of 100g components that would finally fit, or worse when machining parts.

So I started Hanomi, where we are doing something about it. S-Hawk is the studio that holds whatever comes next.

This page is a draft. It will grow.
