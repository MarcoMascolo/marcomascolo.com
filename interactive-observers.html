Companion to part three. The whole box is one static block of spacetime: the floor is space, up is time, and the pale diagonal walls are the light cone. Two people live in it, A and B. Each has a coloured slice, their "now." Speed them up toward light speed, sweep time, and drop a mass in to bend the slices. Watch how their nows tilt, freeze, and bend differently.

This is an illustration, not a literal simulation. Space runs along the floor, time runs up, light moves at 45 degrees. A person's "now" is the coloured slice of events they call this instant. Tune it and watch.
