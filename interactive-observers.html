Companion to part three. This is the honest, rigorous version of the "two people, two spinning cross sections" idea: a spacetime diagram with one space axis and one time axis. It is a 2D stand in for your real situation (a 3D slice of a 4D world), because a faithful 4D picture cannot be drawn. Everything here is real special relativity, just one dimension down. Other demos: slicing 1D to 4D.

When do two people share a now?

The grey square is the static block: space runs across, time runs up, and the diagonal grey lines are the light cone (speed of light). Two people are two worldlines. Each carries a "now," a slice of simultaneity that tilts with their speed. Drive their speeds and sweep time, and watch when their nows line up, when they meet, and what happens near light speed.
